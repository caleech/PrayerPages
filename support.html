Frequently Asked Questions

Before contacting support, check our FAQs below. Your question may already be answered.

How does FaithfulRosary determine which mystery to show each day?

FaithfulRosary follows the traditional weekly cycle for mysteries: Joyful (Monday and Saturday), Sorrowful (Tuesday and Friday), Glorious (Wednesday and Sunday), and Luminous (Thursday).

Does FaithfulRosary require an internet connection?

No, FaithfulRosary is designed to work offline, making it a perfect companion for quiet, disconnected prayer time.

What languages does FaithfulRosary support?

Currently, FaithfulRosary is available in English only. We're working to include more languages in future updates.
